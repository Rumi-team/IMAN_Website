IMAN is the Iranian-American Muslim Association of North America, serving the Los Angeles Iranian-American community since 1990. We offer weekly classes, marriage and memorial services, and daily congregational prayers. Our center is a home for faith, culture, and community gatherings that honor both Islamic traditions and Persian heritage.

Design System

Color Palette

پالت رنگ

Every color is derived from Persian architectural materials: tilework glazes, carved stone, illuminated manuscripts, and the natural landscape of Iranian gardens.

Isfahan Turquoise
#1B6B7D
Primary accent. The dominant haft-rang tilework glaze.
Lapis Lazuli
#1E4D8F
Depth and structure. From unpolished lapis pigment.
Illumination Gold
#C4963C
Ceremonial accent. Tazhib manuscript gilding.
Kashan Plaster
#F4EDE4
Primary background. Warm lime plaster in afternoon light.
Parchment
#FFF9EF
Surface/cards. Aged manuscript paper.
Lapis Ink
#1E2A30
Primary text. Blue-charcoal, never pure black.
Persimmon Ceramic
#E85D3A
Hot accent. Safavid tile terracotta. Use sparingly.
Garden Cypress
#2D5F4E
Sophisticated near-black green. NOT mosque green.
Madder Clay
#8E3B2F
Error/danger state. From madder root dye.
Carved Stone
#D7C7AC
Dividers and borders. Sandstone relief texture.
